Chesapeake Bay: Dissolved Oxygen Analysis

This analysis was completed as a semester long project for an intro data science and analytics class. It goes through the full data science life cycle in an attempt to better understand dissolved oxygen levels within the water of the Chesapeake Bay. The analysis uses Chesapeake Bay water quality data from 2007-2020 to find trends and create predictive models in order to try to understand when and why low level dissolved oxygen level events occur.
